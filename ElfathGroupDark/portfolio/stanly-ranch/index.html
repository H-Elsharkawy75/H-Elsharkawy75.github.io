Stanly Ranch

In Development

Stanly Ranch is an Auberge Resort property and residential offering in Napa Valley, California, currently in construction by SRGA LP. Located on the pristine rolling vineyards of the historic 712-acre Stanly Ranch, the development will include a 135-room Auberge Resort & Spa, 70 Vineyard homes, 40 Villa homes and winery. Anticipated to open in 2021, SRGA LP has retained S&P to advise on the development design and offering while developing and executing the marketing and sales of this distinct destination in the Carneros Region of wine country.

Continuing to specialize in urban super prime real estate and properties of distinction on the West Coast and Hawaii, S&P has built decades of experience representing world-class recreational properties in the most coveted destinations around the globe. Building on a client relationship for over a decade, Stanly Ranch is a natural addition to our California portfolio as one of the most highly sought-after recreational offerings with storied luxury hospitality brand, Auberge Resorts. With Napa quickly emerging as the premier drive to destination from the bustle of the Bay Area, S&P looks forward to unveiling Stanly Ranch as California’s next great resort property.
