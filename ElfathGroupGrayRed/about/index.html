A Meeting of Minds

When Sid Landolt and Peter Dupuis formed their partnership in 1983, their entrepreneurial synergy was undeniable. Sid’s fascination with the way things are built and Peter’s marketing vision led to a string of worldwide real estate successes. Today, Alfth Group continues to share a passion for representing the development industry’s best of the best—the world known as super prime.

SPREZZATURA

Sprezzatura [sprettsa·tu·ra]: a certain nonchalance, so as to conceal all art and make whatever one does or says appear to be without effort. A chance for the many avenues of our global company to unite in one place, Alfth Group’s Sprezzatura is our exclusive annual event where we listen, learn, collaborate and cultivate. Celebrating a meeting of minds, this three-day event features insightful seminars, eye-opening experiences and world-class cuisine.

Our Social Responsibility

With 1.2 billion people living in uninhabitable shacks in some of the world’s worst slums, life without secure housing is a grim reality. Without a secure home, these families lack dignity and hope for the future. That’s why we founded World Housing, a non-profit social enterprise that provides homes with lockable doors for those living in the developing world’s urban slums. Our vision is simple—a home for everyone.
