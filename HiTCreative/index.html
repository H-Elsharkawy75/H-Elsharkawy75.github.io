About us

WHO WE ARE

Hit Creative communications is a Cairo based agency that was established in 2003. An award winning agency with over 30 awarded productions, HIT is famous for its fully integrated communication solutions that serve businesses across the Middle East. We endeavour to create cutting-edge and out-of-the-box advertising solutions that enable all our clients to reach their respective goals successfully and glamorously, whatever their business volume may be.
Our numerous services include: advertising campaigns, POS, packaging, product launching, brand identity and communications through social media, apps, activations and more.
