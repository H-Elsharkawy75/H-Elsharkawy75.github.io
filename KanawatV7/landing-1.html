Why Us

When partnering with Kanawat you can be assured that you are getting comprehensive market coverage. We take pride in our partnerships and we will always manage your products as our own. We will provide you with the most suitable value proposition to your business and the most effective sales & distribution strategy to suit your business goals.

A key strength at Kanawat is our understanding that each business is different and therefore we will always strive to offer each business innovative, unique ideas to deal with your distribution needs.

Our Mission

To work progressively toward redefining the distribution process by bringing in technology products and solutions, fulfilling ongoing market demand and by adding value to both vendor and reseller through timely availability of the goods, product knowledge and value-adding to a profitable working relationship. To work effectively towards creating demand for products and passage to market for vendor and by the generation of beneficial opportunities for our reseller partner by the creation of effective rewards programme, efficient supply chain and knowledge by people training.

Our Vision

Kanawat Co. endeavours to be the leading distributor of choice in Egypt.

Our Shareholders

BPE Partners and MTI have partnered to form Kanawat.

MTI controls 70.5% of Kanawat’s equity.

MTI is a publicly traded joint stock company that was founded in 1992 and is one of Egypt’s largest distribution corporations, with operations in both distribution and retail. It is listed on the Egyptian stock Exchange.

BPE Partners (formerly Beltone Equity) is a private equity venture capital manager fund based in Egypt that was founded in 2006.

BPE Partners invests in a variety of high-growth sectors of the of the Egyptian economy, and BPE provides the management support needed to accelerate and maximize the potential of these investments.

BPE Partners have a diverse portfolio of investments in a variety of industries.

BPE partners have a diversified set of investments across distinct sectors:

- real estate development,

- downstream oil and gas,

- IT system integration,

- non-banking financial services,

- renewable energy,

- manufacturing and food & beverage.
