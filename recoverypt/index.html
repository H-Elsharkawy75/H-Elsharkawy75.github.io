At Recovery PT (RPT) we believe in a concierge approach to treatment. Every aspect of your experience with us is personalized around your specific needs, preferences, and goals as a patient. Owned and managed by Physical Therapists, our core team has been working together for more than 25 years.

As a Recovery PT patient, you’ll have access to unique patient benefits including convenient online scheduling, web-based home exercise programs, and use of fully-equipped NY Sports Club facilities during your treatment.

Every aspect of our business is patient-centric; from our easy online scheduling requests to our 3D-animated and customized home exercise programs (HEP) that can be easily accessed, via the web, from home, while at work or traveling.

Head Pain

At your initial appointment, one of our highly trained physical therapists will conduct a physical evaluation and ask you questions about your health history and symptoms, in order to create a custom treatment plan.

Shoulder Pain

Shoulder pain can present itself in many ways. Maybe you feel pain in your shoulder when you’re trying to get in a comfortable sleeping position, when you swim, when you throw a ball, or when you reach to your top cabinet to grab a glass. However your shoulder pain may present itself, it’s no secret that it physically limits your life.

learn more >>

Back Pain

Back pain is one of the most common conditions seen by physicians across the country. It is said that over 80% of people will suffer some sort of low back pain during their lifetime. Back pain can occur for a variety of reasons including poor motion and mobility, spinal, abdominal or hip weakness.

learn more >>

Hip Pain

Hip pain is typically felt in 3 places, the groin, outer hip or deep buttock. Depending on where the pain is focused corresponds to the dysfunction around that area. The hip joint is incredible as it moves through a large range of motion, while bearing the weight of the body and providing stability.

learn more >>
